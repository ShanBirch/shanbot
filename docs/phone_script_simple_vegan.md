### Simple Phone Script (Vegan Audience)

- **1) Warm open**
  - “Hey [Name], Shannon from Coco’s. Still good to chat?”

- **2) Purpose check**
  - “What made you sign up—health, energy, ethical, or performance goals?”

- **3) Deep ‘Why’ loop**
  - “Why is [their vegan/fitness goal] important now?”
  - “What would hitting it let you do? Why that?”
  - “How’s this been affecting you? For how long?”

- **4) Current approach**
  - “What are you doing now—training and vegan nutrition?”
  - “How long? Why that approach?”
  - “Do you like your plan?” If yes: “What do you like?”

- **5) Reason to change**
  - “Besides [goal], why streamline now vs keep experimenting solo?”

- **6) SMART goal**
  - “Where are you now? Ideal weight/strength/energy by when?”

- **7) Lifestyle + budget scan (vegan‑aware)**
  - Groceries/out‑of‑home: whole foods vs mock meats, eating out, delivery
  - FASTERB + vegan spend (supps, cafes). “Open to small shifts for results? Why?”

- **8) Support & stress**
  - Partner/family supportive of vegan goals? Any pushback?
  - Finances okay to start?

- **9) Schedule & commitment**
  - “AM or PM? 3–4 sessions/week workable?” Lock times.

- **10) Solution awareness**
  - “Were you looking for vegan‑savvy coaching? What stopped you before?”
  - If tried: “How did it go? What held you back?”

- **11) Ideal criteria**
  - “Besides [X] & [Y], what must a program have for vegan success? Why now?”

- **12) Future vision**
  - “If you hit [goal] in 16 weeks, what changes first?”
  - “What would that do for you personally?”

- **13) Consequences**
  - “If nothing changes in 3–12 months, are you okay with that?”
  - “Do you want change or stay as is?”

- **14) Transition**
  - “Based on what you’ve shared, our vegan‑friendly coaching could fit. Quick run‑through?”

- **15) Presentation (6 weeks, $599)**
  - Share 3–4 vegan‑specific items:
    - Feature → Benefit → Problem it solves
  - Temp checks: “Make sense?” “With me?” “How’s that feel?”

- **16) Commit (close)**
  - “Feel like this could be your answer? Why?”
  - “What part feels most valuable for you?”

- **17) The ask (next step)**
  - “Next step: secure your spot for [program]. Card works. Then onboarding: goals, vegan nutrition setup, first session time. Sound good—or another way you’d like to proceed?”

- **18) Payment details**
  - BSB / Account (if bank transfer) or card now; lock onboarding time.

### Vegan plug‑and‑play features

- Vegan macro setup (protein‑forward) → balanced meals → fat loss without hunger.
- Recipe packs (whole‑food + high‑protein) → quick tasty options → adherence.
- Supplement guidance (B12, D3, DHA/EPA, creatine) → energy & recovery → no guesswork.
- Gym & home templates → progressive overload → strength and tone.
- Weekly coach check‑ins → adjustments & accountability → steady, visible progress.
- Messaging support → fast answers on menus/travel → stay on track socially.

- “What’s been hardest about hitting protein on a vegan diet?”
- “Do social meals make it tricky—what happens there?”
- “Mock meats vs whole foods—what’s your balance now?”
- “How are energy and recovery after training?”
- “Any supplements right now? B12, D3, omega‑3, creatine?”
