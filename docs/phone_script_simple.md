### Simple Phone Script (Cheat Sheet)

- **1) Warm open**
  - “Hey [Name], Shannon from Coco’s. Still good to chat?”

- **2) Purpose check**
  - “What made you sign up?”

- **3) Deep ‘Why’ loop**
  - “Why is [their goal] important?”
  - “What would [goal] let you do? Why that?”
  - “How’s that affected you? For how long?”
  - Loop until a clear emotional reason emerges.

- **4) Current approach**
  - “What are you doing now?”
  - “How long? Why that?”
  - “Do you like your plan?” If yes: “What do you like?”

- **5) Reason to change**
  - “Besides [their goal], why streamline now vs same old?”

- **6) Define SMART goal**
  - “Where are you now? Where do you want to be by when?”

- **7) Lifestyle + budget scan (FASTERB + self‑care)**
  - Food/drink: Fast food, Alcohol, Coffee, Tobacco, Entertainment, Restaurants
  - Beauty/hobbies spend; “Open to reducing a bit for your goal? Why?”

- **8) Support & stress check**
  - Partner aware/supportive?
  - Finances okay enough to start?
  - Light, curious tone.

- **9) Schedule & commitment**
  - “AM or PM? 3–4 sessions/week workable?” Lock preferred times.

- **10) Solution awareness**
  - “Were you looking for options? What stopped you before?”
  - If tried: “How did it go? What held you back?”

- **11) Ideal criteria**
  - “Besides [X] & [Y], what must a program have? Why now?”

- **12) Future vision (benefits)**
  - “If you hit [goal] in 16 weeks, what changes first?”
  - “What would that do for you personally?” Probe feelings.

- **13) Consequences (do nothing)**
  - “What if nothing changes in 3–12 months? Okay with that?”
  - “Do you want change or stay as is?”

- **14) Transition**
  - “Based on what you’ve shared, what we do could fit. Want a quick run‑through?”

- **15) Presentation (6 weeks, $599)**
  - Share 3–4 items in this format:
    - Feature → Benefit → Problem it solves
  - Mini temp checks: “Make sense?” “With me?” “How’s that feel?”

- **16) Commit (close)**
  - “Feel like this could be your answer? Why?”
  - “What part feels most valuable?”

- **17) The ask (next step)**
  - “Next step: secure your spot for [program]. Card works. Then we onboard: goals, plan, first session time. Sound good—or another way you’d like to proceed?”

- **18) Payment details**
  - BSB / Account (if bank transfer) or card now; lock onboarding time.

### 30‑second version

1) Why now?  
2) Real reason (feelings)?  
3) Current plan & gaps?  
4) Must‑haves?  
5) If nothing changes—okay with that?  
6) Our 6‑week $599 fixes your gaps (3–4 points).  
7) Feels right? Card + onboarding time.

---

### 5 power questions

- “What made you book this call today, specifically?”
- “Why is [goal] important—what changes first if you get it?”
- “What’s your current approach, and what’s not working?”
- “What must a program include for you to succeed now?”
- “If we start today, are AM/PM slots and 3–4/week realistic?”

---

### Plug‑and‑play features

- Unlimited coached sessions → perfect form, right weights → fewer plateaus/injuries.
- Personal nutrition plan → simple meals you enjoy → no guesswork or yo‑yoing.
- Weekly check‑ins → accountability & tweaks → steady, visible progress.
- Messaging support → quick help → small issues don’t derail you.

### Objection nudges

- **Time**: “Which times work best this week—AM or PM?”
- **Money**: “What could we trim short‑term to fund your goal?”
- **Partner**: “Want to loop them in now so they feel part of the win?”
- **Confidence**: “What support would make this feel doable from day one?”
