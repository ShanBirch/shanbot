ðŸŽ¨ Ad Portfolio Strategy

Run These 5 Ad Types Simultaneously:

1. Your Current Winner (40% of budget) - Keep your "Looking for 6 Vegans" ad running - This is your proven performer - maintain it!

2. Success Story Carousel (20% of budget) - 3-4 slides of client transformations - Include quotes and specific results - "See what 42 days can do..."

3. Behind-the-Scenes Video (15% of budget) - You preparing vegan meals or training - 15-30 seconds showing your expertise - Builds personal connection

4. Educational Static Ad (15% of budget) - "5 Vegan Weight Loss Mistakes" format - Positions you as the expert - Drives curiosity and engagement

5. Weekly Challenge Ad (10% of budget) - "New challenge starting Monday - 6 spots available" - Creates consistent urgency without being pushy - Always truthful about weekly availability

ðŸš¨ Red Flags & Immediate Actions

If Cost Per DM > $6: - Pause ad immediately - Review targeting (too broad?) - Check ad relevance score

If CTR < 1%: - Creative fatigue - refresh images/video - Test new headlines - Adjust targeting to more specific interests

If Frequency > 4: - Expand audience size - Introduce new creative variations - Reduce daily budget temporarily
